About Me

Multidisciplinary engineer with 9+ years of experience in the modeling, simulation, and optimization of chemical and biomedical systems. Proficient in 3D CAD, FEA, and CFD for the design and analysis of complex systems. Demonstrated ability to convert simulation insights into experimental designs, prototypes, and lab-to-pilot scale systems. Skilled in data-driven optimization, design of experiments (DOE), and integrating machine learning into model-based scale-up strategies. Strong track record of collaboration with leading EU partners including VITO, IMEC, John Cockerill, and Johnson & Johnson. Ph.D. mechanical engineer with proven expertise in delivering computational solutions for electrodialysis, water electrolyzers, fuel cells, and membrane systems that achieve measurable performance improvements of up to 30% efficiency gains.
