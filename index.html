About Me

I am a Post-Doctoral Researcher specializing in Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) and electrochemical systems with over 9 years of hands-on research experience. My expertise lies in numerical modeling, simulation, and optimization of sustainable energy technologies including PEM fuel cells, alkaline water electrolyzers, and Li-ion batteries.
