About Me

Post-Doc Mechanical Engineer with 10+ years of expertise in multiphysics CFD simulations for electrochemical systems. Currently working as a researcher at KU Leuven, I specialize in developing computational solutions for electrodialysis, water electrolyzers, fuel cells, and membrane systems.

My research, with 594 citations, has delivered measurable performance improvements including up to 30% efficiency gains in electrochemical systems. I have extensive experience collaborating with leading research institutions including VITO, imec, John Cockerill, and TU Braunschweig.
