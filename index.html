About Me

Multidisciplinary mechanical engineer with 9+ years of experience in modeling, simulation, and optimization of electrochemical and biomedical systems. Proficient in 3D CAD, FEA, and CFD for design and analysis of complex systems. Demonstrated ability to convert simulation insights into experimental designs, prototypes, and lab-to-pilot scale systems. Skilled in data-driven optimization, design of experiments (DOE), and integrating machine learning into model-based scale-up strategies. Strong track record of collaboration with leading EU partners including VITO, imec, John Cockerill, and Johnson & Johnson.

Research Focus

My work bridges theoretical modeling and experimental validation in electrochemical energy systems. I develop detailed CFD models to optimize fuel cell performance, improve electrolyzer efficiency, and advance membrane separation systems. Currently focused on alkaline water electrolyzers for green hydrogen production, electrodialysis systems for lithium recovery, and membrane technology for medical and industrial applications.
