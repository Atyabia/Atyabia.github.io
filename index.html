About Me

Multidisciplinary engineer with 9+ years of experience in the modeling, simulation, and optimization of chemical and biomedical systems. Proficient in 3D CAD, FEA, and CFD for the design and analysis of complex system. Demonstrated ability to convert simulation insights into experimental designs, prototypes, and lab-to-pilot scale systems. Skilled in data-driven optimization, design of experiments (DOE), and integrating machine learning into model-based scale-up strategies. Strong track record of collaboration with leading EU partners—including VITO, IMEC, John Cockerill, and Johnson & Johnson.

Research Focus

My work bridges theoretical modeling and experimental validation in electrochemical energy systems. I develop detailed CFD models to optimize fuel cell performance, improve electrolyzer efficiency, and advance battery thermal management solutions. Currently focused on alkaline water electrolyzers for green hydrogen production, CO2 electrolyzers for sustainable carbon capture, and electrodialysis systems for advanced separation processes.
