About Me

I am a passionate mechanical engineer and post-doctoral researcher with over 10 years of experience in CFD simulation and electrochemical systems. My research focuses on PEM fuel cells, alkaline water electrolyzers, and sustainable energy technologies. I specialize in multiphysics modeling using COMSOL and ANSYS, combining computational expertise with practical laboratory experience.

üî¨ Research Interests

My work bridges theoretical modeling and experimental validation in electrochemical energy systems.
